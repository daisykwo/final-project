About Me

Hi! I'm Daisy!

I grew up in San Francisco, but I'm currently a second-year at UC San Diego. I am majoring in chemistry and minoring in business. Although I have a great passion for STEM, I also love to create. I started taking studio art classes at the age of 4 and I fall in love with the medium. Mainly, I focus creating hyperrealism paintings in oil, but I also have a couple of recreations of classic paintings as well as more modern paintings. I love learning and exploring new mediums, and this fall, I took a intro to coding class at UCSD and I also wanted to share some works the class. Enjoy!
